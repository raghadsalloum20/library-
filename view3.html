Descreption: "Benighted," also known as "The Old Dark House," is a 1927 novel by J.B. Priestley. The story revolves around a group of travelers who seek refuge in an eerie and dilapidated mansion during a storm, only to encounter its strange and unsettling inhabitants. Blending elements of gothic horror and dark comedy, the novel explores themes of isolation, fear, and the human condition.
